Korean Air Sets December 2026 Launch Date for Integrated Carrier After Asiana Merger

Korean Air and Asiana Airlines confirmed Wednesday that the two carriers will complete their full legal merger on December 17, 2026, creating South Korea's integrated flag carrier. Korean Air will absorb all of Asiana's assets, liabilities, rights and personnel at a merger ratio of 1 Korean Air share to 0.2736432 Asiana shares. The landmark consolidation follows a 2020 share subscription agreement and was underpinned by 3.6 trillion won in government-backed liquidity support provided to Asiana during the COVID-19 pandemic.
