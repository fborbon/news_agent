Singapore's Ministry of Defence has launched SG Defence, a new volunteer platform consolidating 10 civilian- and military-linked volunteer schemes under one network, open to all residents aged 16 and above, including permanent residents and foreigners. Singapore Defends Pragmatic Human Rights Approach at UN Universal Periodic Review

Minister of State Rahayu Mahzam told a UN Universal Periodic Review session in Geneva on May 12 that Singapore takes a pragmatic, outcome-focused approach to human rights, rejecting a one-size-fits-all model. She cited improvements in life expectancy — from 64 years at independence in 1965 to 84 years today — near-universal school enrolment, and Singapore's third-place ranking in Transparency International's 2025 Corruption Perceptions Index. The UPR, held every five years for all 193 UN member states, assessed Singapore's report covering worker discrimination protections and family support programmes. Ms Rahayu acknowledged that global pressures including geopolitical tensions and technology were straining social unity worldwide.
