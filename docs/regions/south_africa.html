Ramaphosa Unveils R1 Trillion Infrastructure Plan to Fight Unemployment

President Cyril Ramaphosa announced a sweeping R1 trillion infrastructure investment programme at the South Africa Infrastructure Investment Summit in Cape Town on Wednesday.
